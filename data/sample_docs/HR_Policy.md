# HR Policy Document — TechCorp India
## Version 2.1 | Effective: January 2024

---

## 1. Leave Policy

### 1.1 Annual Leave (Paid Time Off)
All full-time employees are eligible for **24 paid leaves annually**. These leaves are credited at the beginning of each calendar year and can be carried forward for a maximum of 12 days into the next year.

### 1.2 Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to **12 sick leaves per year**. Sick leave requires a medical certificate if taken for more than 3 consecutive days. Unused sick leave cannot be carried forward.

### 1.3 Casual Leave
Each employee receives **6 casual leaves per year** for personal matters. Casual leaves must be pre-approved by the reporting manager with at least 24 hours notice, except in emergencies.

### 1.4 Maternity & Paternity Leave
- Maternity leave: **26 weeks** (as per Maternity Benefit Act, 1961)
- Paternity leave: **5 working days** within 6 months of childbirth

### 1.5 National and Festival Holidays
Employees receive **10 national/festival holidays** per year as per the company's official holiday calendar published each January.

---

## 2. Work from Home Policy

Employees may work from home up to **2 days per week** subject to manager approval. Work from home eligibility begins after completion of 6 months of employment. Employees must maintain core working hours (10 AM – 4 PM IST) during WFH days.

---

## 3. Performance Review and Appraisal

Performance reviews are conducted **twice annually** — in April and October. Salary increments are processed in **April** each year, effective from the 1st of April. Increment ranges:
- Exceeds Expectations: 15–25%
- Meets Expectations: 8–14%
- Needs Improvement: 0–7%

---

## 4. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:
- Maintain professional behavior in all work communications
- Protect company confidential information
- Report conflicts of interest to the HR department
- Avoid use of company resources for personal gain

Violations of the code of conduct can result in disciplinary action, including termination.

---

## 5. Travel and Expense Policy

### 5.1 Domestic Travel
Business travel requires pre-approval from the department head. Reimbursable expenses include:
- Economy airfare or AC 2-tier train tickets
- Hotel up to ₹4,000/night in Tier-1 cities, ₹2,500/night in Tier-2 cities
- Daily food allowance: ₹600

### 5.2 International Travel
International travel must be approved by the VP and HR. Per diem rates vary by country (refer to the International Travel Rate Card on the intranet).

---

## 6. Separation and Notice Period

- **Probationary employees:** 1 month notice period
- **Confirmed employees:** 3 months notice period
- Buyout of notice period is permitted with approval at 1x monthly CTC

---

## 7. Grievance Redressal

Employees can raise grievances through:
1. Direct conversation with reporting manager
2. HR Business Partner (hrpartner@techcorp.in)
3. Anonymous grievance portal at intranet.techcorp.in/grievance

All grievances are addressed within **15 working days** of receipt.
