# Customer Policy & Support Guide
## TechCorp Products — Customer Service Standards

---

## 1. Refund Policy

### 1.1 Standard Refund Window
Customers are eligible for a full refund within **30 days of purchase** for any product or subscription, no questions asked. After 30 days, refund eligibility is assessed on a case-by-case basis.

### 1.2 Digital Products & Software Licenses
For software licenses and digital downloads:
- Full refund available within **7 days** if the product was not downloaded/activated
- Partial refund (50%) available between 7–30 days if technical issues are verified
- No refund after 30 days for digital products that have been activated

### 1.3 Subscription Services
Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Refunds are prorated for unused days in the current billing period if cancelled within 7 days of renewal. Annual subscriptions:
- Full refund within 30 days of annual renewal
- 50% refund between 30–90 days
- No refund after 90 days

### 1.4 Refund Process
To initiate a refund:
1. Contact support at support@techcorp.in or call 1800-XXX-XXXX
2. Provide Order ID and reason for refund
3. Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** to the original payment method

---

## 2. Warranty Policy

All hardware products come with a **1-year manufacturer warranty** covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty (2 additional years) can be purchased within 90 days of the original purchase.

Warranty does not cover:
- Physical damage caused by misuse
- Water damage
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications

## 3. Support Tiers

### Tier 1 — Standard Support (All customers)
- Email support: Response within **24 business hours**

### Tier 2 — Business Support (Business plan customers)

### Tier 3 — Enterprise Support (Enterprise plan customers)
- **24/7 phone and email** support

## 4. Data Privacy Policy

TechCorp collects and processes customer data in accordance with the **Information Technology Act, 2000** and applicable data protection regulations.

- Customer data is never sold to third parties
- Data is retained for **7 years** as per regulatory requirements
- Customers can request data deletion by emailing privacy@techcorp.in
- Data breach notification will be sent within **72 hours** of discovery

---

## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

Customers must not use TechCorp products for:
- Any illegal or fraudulent activity
- Spam or unsolicited communications
- Circumventing security measures
- Intellectual property violations

Violations may result in immediate account suspension without refund.
